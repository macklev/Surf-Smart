Surf Smart

Inspiring kids to Surf Smart and educating on cyber safety.

What do you know about surfing smart?

Based on your age group, choose a game to check your knowledge of how to be safe online!

6-8 Year Olds

Play a fun game where you choose the photo of who you should tell in a situation.

9-10 Year Olds

Play a fun game where you drag actions into safe, careful, or dangerous categories.

11-12 Year Olds

Play a fun quiz game where you answer quick internet safety questions.

Are you a parent?

Educating your childing on cyber safety extremely important. In a time where cyber threats are as big as ever, and children are growing up in a world centered online, knowing how to keep them safe is essential.
